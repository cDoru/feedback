The Feedback Card

A simple way to provide feedback

The Feedback Card was created after an inspirational talk on feedback in an organization. Employees in modern organizations often forget to continuously provide feedback for each other. This tool is meant to empower people with an easy process of providing constructive feedback.

Whether the feedback is good or it focuses on improvement points, it is important. The feedback here is divided into four simple parts: The situation, the observation, the feeling, and the advice. This information is then compiled into an email. The email itself is not sent by finishing the steps. The final move is to open an email where the written text has been added and formatted. By using a website it might become easier for the employee to actually get the feedback to the receiver.
