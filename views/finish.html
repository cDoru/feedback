Send your feedback


The button below has created an email layout for you to easily send feedback to your colleague. The mail itself will not send immediately when you click the button. It will open your preferred email and show you the result it has generated. Then it is up to you to actually send your email.

Remember it is always a good idea to follow up on your feedback with a face to face chat. This goes for both positive feedback and suggestions for improvement.
